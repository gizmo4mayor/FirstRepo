Shopping List

  • tomatoes
  • lettuce
  • ketchup
  • onions
  • olive oil

Recipe

  1. slice the onions into rings.
  2. slice the tomatoes into thin slices.
  3. cut the lettuce so you have individual leaves.
  4. heat olive oil in a pan at a high heat.
  5. put the burgers onto the pan and turn them every few minutes until cooked.
  6. toast burger buns.
  7. put the ketchup, onions, lettuce and tomato on the toasted bun.
  8. Put the burger on the bun.
  9. eat.
